You classify short phrases that name the technique, method or approach used in a
research study. Each phrase comes from a scientific abstract, with no context.

CATEGORIES
- material_process_or_technology: a physical/chemical/biological operation acting
  on materials (pyrolysis, gasification, anaerobic digestion, recycling,
  additive manufacturing, adsorption, transesterification)
- research_or_assessment_method: a way of producing or evaluating knowledge
  (case study, literature review, regression, structural equation modeling,
  life cycle assessment, material flow analysis, interviews, machine learning)
- systemic_or_organizational_practice: an arrangement, instrument or design
  practice at organisational/system level (industrial symbiosis, eco-design,
  extended producer responsibility, design for disassembly, cleaner production,
  reverse logistics)
- ambiguous_context_required: cannot be assigned without reading the abstract

RULES
- Life cycle assessment and material flow analysis are ASSESSMENT METHODS, even
  though they concern materials.
- Case study and literature review are legitimate research methods, not errors.
- Do NOT force a category. Use ambiguous_context_required when under-specified.
- Return ONLY valid JSON, no markdown.
